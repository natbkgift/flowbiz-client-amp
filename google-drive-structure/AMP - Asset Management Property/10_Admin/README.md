## Overview

This is the administrative hub - team contacts, vendor information, and general administrative files that don't fit in other categories.

## Structure

### Team/
Team-related information and contacts:

**Required Files** (Create these):

**Contact_List.xlsx**
- All team members
- Columns: Name, Role, Phone, Email, LINE ID, Emergency Contact
- Keep updated

**Team_Directory.docx**
- Detailed team information
- Roles and responsibilities
- Work schedule
- Areas of expertise
- Photo directory (optional)

### Vendors_Partners/
Information about external partners:

**Categories to track:**
- Photographers/Videographers
- Marketing agencies
- Contractors (maintenance, repairs)
- Legal services
- Accounting services
- Property developers
- Other real estate agents

**For each vendor create:**
`Vendor_[Name].docx` including:
- Company name
- Contact person
- Phone/Email/LINE
- Services provided
- Pricing/Rates
- Quality rating
- Notes/Reviews

### Archive/
Old administrative files:
- Outdated contact lists
- Old vendor information
- Historical records
- Superseded documents

## Essential Information

### Team Contact List

Should include:
- Full name
- Role/Position
- Phone (primary and backup)
- Email
- LINE ID
- Emergency contact
- Start date
- Languages spoken
- Areas of expertise

### Vendor Management

**Maintaining good vendor relationships:**

1. **Track quality** - Rate their work
2. **Negotiate rates** - Especially for repeat business
3. **Keep alternatives** - Don't rely on single vendors
4. **Pay promptly** - Good vendors are worth keeping
5. **Give feedback** - Help them improve

**Recommended vendors to have:**
- Professional photographer (properties)
- Drone operator (aerial shots)
- Videographer (property tours)
- Graphic designer (marketing materials)
- Translator (Thai-English)
- Lawyer (contract review)
- Accountant (tax, finance)
- Website developer
- Social media manager

## Workflow

### New Team Member

1. Add to Contact_List.xlsx
2. Update Team_Directory.docx
3. Add photo (if used)
4. Set up system access
5. Assign mentor/buddy
6. Start onboarding (see 08_Operations/Training/)

### New Vendor

1. Get complete information
2. Create Vendor_[Name].docx
3. Test their service (trial project)
4. Rate and review
5. Add to preferred vendor list if good

### Quarterly Review

- [ ] Update Contact_List (any changes?)
- [ ] Review vendor performance
- [ ] Remove outdated information
- [ ] Archive old files
- [ ] Verify emergency contacts

## Tips

- **Keep contacts current** - Outdated info is useless
- **Build vendor relationships** - They're part of your team
- **Document everything** - Don't rely on memory
- **Regular backups** - Contact info is critical
- **Emergency preparedness** - Always have backup contacts
