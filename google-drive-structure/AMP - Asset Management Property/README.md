## Overview

This is the complete Google Drive folder structure for AMP (Asset Management Property) as specified in the documentation.

**To upload to Google Drive:**
1. Upload the entire folder structure to your Google Drive
2. Share with team members according to access permissions
3. Set up appropriate folder permissions as documented

## Quick Reference

### Main Folders

1. **01_Properties/** - All property listings (new projects by type)
2. **02_Resale_Secondary/** - Resale/secondary market properties
3. **03_Rental/** - Long-term and short-term rental properties
4. **04_Leads_CRM/** - Lead tracking and customer relationship management
5. **05_Marketing/** - Marketing materials, campaigns, and brand assets
6. **06_LINE_Group_Summary/** - Daily summaries from LINE groups
7. **07_Contracts_Legal/** - Contract templates and legal documents
8. **08_Operations/** - SOPs, checklists, and operational documents
9. **09_Finance/** - Financial tracking and documents
10. **10_Admin/** - Team information and administrative files
