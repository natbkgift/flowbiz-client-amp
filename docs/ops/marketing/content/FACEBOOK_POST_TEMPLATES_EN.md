## CHARACTER COUNT & OPTIMIZATION

### Optimal Post Length

**Facebook Feed Posts:**
- Ideal: 40-80 words (higher engagement)
- Maximum before "See More": 2-3 lines
- Total length: Up to 63,206 characters (but don't!)

**Best Practice:**
```
Hook (1-2 lines) - Grab attention
[VISIBLE ABOVE FOLD]

Details (bullet points) - Easy to scan
[CLICK "SEE MORE"]

Call-to-Action - What to do next
Contact Info - How to reach you
Hashtags - At the end
```

### Emoji Strategy

**Use emojis for:**
- Visual breaks (easier reading)
- Highlighting key points
- Adding personality
- International understanding

**Don't overuse:**
- Max 15-20 emojis per post
- 1-2 per line maximum
- Match emoji to content
- Professional tone still important
