## Writing Tips

### Do's âœ…

1. **Be Descriptive but Honest**
   - Paint a picture with words
   - Don't exaggerate or mislead
   - Use specific details

2. **Focus on Benefits**
   - What's in it for the buyer?
   - How will it improve their life?
   - Why is it special?

3. **Use Power Words**
   - Stunning, breathtaking, elegant
   - Spacious, modern, luxurious
   - Convenient, perfect, ideal

4. **Include Numbers**
   - Exact sizes (sqm)
   - Specific distances (10 min walk)
   - Concrete prices

5. **Address Foreign Buyers**
   - Mention foreign ownership clearly
   - Explain transfer process
   - Include English-speaking service

### Don'ts âŒ

1. **Avoid Vague Language**
   - âŒ "Near beach" â†’ âœ… "5 min walk to beach"

   - Explain or avoid: Rai, Wah, Talang Wah
   - Use international units (SQM)
   - Write dates: YYYY-MM-DD or "January 2026"

3. **Avoid Negative Words**
   - âŒ Don't focus on what it doesn't have
   - âœ… Focus on what it does have
   - Frame positively

   - Keep sentences clear
   - Use simple English
