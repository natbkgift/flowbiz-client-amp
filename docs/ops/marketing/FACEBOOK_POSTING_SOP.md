## Photo & Video Guidelines

### Photo Requirements

**Minimum Standards:**
- Resolution: 1080x1080px or higher
- Format: JPG or PNG
- Quality: Professional, well-lit
- Orientation: Square (1:1) or landscape (4:3)

**Photo Count per Post:**
- Minimum: 3 photos
- Optimal: 5-7 photos
- Maximum: 10 photos

**Photo Order:**
1. Hero shot (best view/feature)
2. Living area
3. Bedroom(s)
4. Bathroom
5. Kitchen
6. View/balcony
7. Building/facilities
8. Location/area

**Photo Editing:**
- Brightness: Increase 10-20%
- Contrast: Subtle adjustment
- Saturation: Slight boost
- Watermark: Add AMP logo (bottom corner, subtle)

### Video Guidelines

**Preferred:**
- Length: 30-90 seconds
- Format: MP4, vertical or square
- Resolution: 1080p minimum
- Content: Property walkthrough
- Include: Text overlays with key info
- Music: Upbeat, copyright-free

**Video Structure:**
```
0-5 sec:   Property exterior/building
5-15 sec:  Living area pan
15-25 sec: Bedroom
25-35 sec: Bathroom
35-50 sec: View/balcony
50-60 sec: Facilities (pool, gym)
60-70 sec: Area/location
70-90 sec: Contact info & CTA
```

- ‚ùå Photos not of the actual property
