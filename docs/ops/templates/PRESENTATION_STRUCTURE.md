## üìê Design Guidelines

### Overall Presentation Style

**Typography:**
- **Headers:** Kanit or Prompt (Thai-friendly)
- **Body:** Sarabun or Sukhumvit Set
- **English:** Helvetica or Open Sans
- **Size:** Headers 32-44pt, Body 18-24pt

**Color Scheme:**
- Use project brand colors as primary
- White/light backgrounds for readability
- Accent colors for highlights
- Consistent throughout deck

**Layout:**
- **Margins:** Minimum 0.5" on all sides
- **Alignment:** Left-aligned text, centered headers
- **White space:** Don't overcrowd slides
- **Rule of thirds:** Balance visuals and text

**Imagery:**
- **Quality:** High-resolution only (min 1920x1080)
- **Consistency:** Same filter/style throughout
- **Source:** Project photos, lifestyle images
- **Avoid:** Generic stock photos

### Slide Consistency

- **Header on every slide** (except cover)
- **Page numbers** (bottom right)
- **Logo** (top or bottom corner)
- **Same font** across all slides
- **Color scheme** consistency
