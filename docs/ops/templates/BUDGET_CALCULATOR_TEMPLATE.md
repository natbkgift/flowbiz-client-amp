## üí° Budget Optimization Tips

### When to Increase Budget

- [ ] CPL below target consistently (2+ weeks)
- [ ] High lead quality feedback from sales
- [ ] Campaign out of learning phase
- [ ] Positive ROI confirmed
- [ ] Audience not saturated (Frequency < 3)

**How to increase:**
- Maximum 20% increase per week
- Monitor for 3-5 days after increase
- Increase best performing campaigns first

### When to Decrease Budget

- [ ] CPL significantly above target (50%+)
- [ ] Poor lead quality
- [ ] High frequency (4+) = ad fatigue
- [ ] Low CTR (< 0.5%)
- [ ] Better opportunities in other channels

**How to decrease:**
- Pause poorest performing ad sets first
- Keep at least 1-2 ad sets running per campaign
- Reallocate to better performing campaigns

### Budget Reallocation Signals

**Move budget TO channel when:**
- Consistently hitting CPL targets
- High lead quality scores
- Room to scale (large audience)
- Strong engagement metrics

**Move budget FROM channel when:**
- CPL 2x above target for 2+ weeks
- Low conversion rates
- Ad fatigue (high frequency, declining CTR)
- Audience saturation

## üìã Budget Planning Checklist

### Pre-Campaign
- [ ] Identify project type and target audience
- [ ] Set realistic CPL target based on benchmarks
- [ ] Calculate required leads for sales target
- [ ] Determine total budget needed
- [ ] Get budget approval from stakeholders
- [ ] Allocate budget across channels
- [ ] Plan production budget (photos, videos, design)
- [ ] Account for tools and software costs

### During Campaign
- [ ] Track spend daily
- [ ] Monitor budget pacing (weekly)
- [ ] Compare actuals vs. targets
- [ ] Identify over/under spending channels
- [ ] Reallocate budget to better performers
- [ ] Adjust bids based on performance
- [ ] Reserve contingency budget (5-10%)

### Post-Campaign
- [ ] Calculate actual CPL by channel
- [ ] Analyze ROI and profitability
- [ ] Document learnings
- [ ] Identify top performing channels
- [ ] Plan next period budget based on data
- [ ] Present results to stakeholders
