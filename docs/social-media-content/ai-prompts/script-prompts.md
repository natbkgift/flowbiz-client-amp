This document provides comprehensive AI prompts for generating video scripts for Asset Management Property's content across various platforms and video lengths. Use these prompts with AI tools like ChatGPT, Claude, or Gemini to create engaging video scripts that drive results.

## How to Use These Prompts

1. **Identify your video type and duration** (Short-form vs. Long-form)
2. **Select the appropriate prompt template** for your needs
3. **Replace all [VARIABLES]** with specific property/campaign details
4. **Generate the script** using your AI tool
5. **Review and customize** for brand voice and accuracy

## Voiceover Guidelines

### Professional Voiceover Specs

**English Voiceover:**
- Accent: Neutral international English or clear British/American
- Tone: Professional but warm, conversational
- Pace: 140-160 words per minute (normal)
- Energy: Mid-to-high energy, enthusiastic but not hyper
- Style: Natural, like explaining to a friend

**Thai Voiceover:**
- Dialect: Central Thai (Bangkok standard)
- Tone: Professional yet friendly
- Pace: Slightly slower for clarity
- Formality: Professional but not overly formal (‡∏Ñ‡∏£‡∏±‡∏ö/‡∏Ñ‡πà‡∏∞ appropriate)
- Style: Trustworthy expert

### Voiceover Direction Notes

For Property Tours:
```
"Keep energy up, like you're excited to show a friend something cool.
Natural enthusiasm when highlighting features.
Conversational pacing - not rushing.
Clear enunciation on prices and important numbers.
Smile in your voice - it comes through."
```

For Educational Content:
```
"Knowledgeable expert sharing helpful information.
Warm and approachable, not lecturing.
Slow down slightly for complex information.
Emphasize key takeaways.
Confident but not arrogant."
```

For Investment/Financial:
```
"Trustworthy, professional tone.
Clear and precise on numbers.
Measured pacing.
Serious but not stuffy.
Inspire confidence through competence."
```

### Recording Technical Requirements

- Format: WAV file, 48kHz, 24-bit
- Environment: Soundproof or treated space
- Microphone: Professional quality (recommended: Rode NT1-A or similar)
- No background noise or echo
- Proper levels: Peak around -6dB, allowing headroom
- Multiple takes of key sections
- Room tone recorded for editing
