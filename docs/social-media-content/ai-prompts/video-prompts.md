# Video Content Creation AI Prompts

## Overview
This comprehensive document provides AI prompts for creating video content strategies, concepts, editing plans, and optimization guides for Asset Management Property's social media presence. Use these prompts to plan, create, and optimize both short-form (TikTok/Reels) and long-form (YouTube) video content.

## How to Use These Prompts

1. **Identify your video format** (Short-form vs. Long-form)
2. **Choose the appropriate content type** (Property tour, Educational, Promotional, etc.)
3. **Select the matching prompt template**
4. **Replace all [VARIABLES]** with your specific information
5. **Generate your video plan** using AI
6. **Review and customize** for your specific needs
7. **Execute production** following the AI-generated framework
8. **Analyze and iterate** based on performance

## Hook Creation Strategies

### The Perfect Hook Framework

```
Create a hook strategy for [VIDEO_TYPE] about [TOPIC] targeting [AUDIENCE].

Hook Psychology Principles:
1. Pattern Interrupt (stop the scroll)
2. Curiosity Gap (they must know more)
3. Value Promise (clear benefit)
4. Relatability (this is for me)
5. Emotion (feel something immediately)

Hook Formats to Consider:

QUESTION HOOK:
"Did you know [surprising fact]?"
"What if I told you [intriguing statement]?"
"Ever wondered [common question]?"

CHALLENGE HOOK:
"Think you can't afford [goal]? Think again."
"Everyone says [common belief]. Here's why they're wrong."

POV HOOK:
"POV: You're viewing a property and realize [something surprising]"

STATEMENT HOOK:
"[Surprising statement about property/market]"
"This condo [surprising attribute]"

STORY HOOK:
"I met a client who [intriguing beginning]..."

NUMBER HOOK:
"3 things I wish I knew before buying in Pattaya"
"The $500,000 mistake 90% of buyers make"

For [YOUR_SPECIFIC_VIDEO], provide:
1. Primary hook (with rationale)
2. 3 alternative hooks for A/B testing
3. First 3 seconds visual strategy
4. Text overlay approach
5. Why each hook works for target audience
6. Which platforms each hook best suits

Make each hook scrolling-stopping powerful.
```
