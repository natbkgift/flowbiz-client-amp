## Platform-Specific Guidelines

### Facebook
- **Character Limit**: No strict limit, but 40-80 characters perform best
- **Optimal Length**: 1-2 paragraphs
- **Best Practices**: Use line breaks, include call-to-action, ask questions
- **Hashtags**: 1-3 hashtags maximum
- **Tone**: Conversational, informative

### Instagram
- **Character Limit**: 2,200 characters (first 125 visible without "more")
- **Optimal Length**: 138-150 characters
- **Best Practices**: Front-load key information, use emojis, storytelling
- **Hashtags**: 10-30 hashtags (place at end or first comment)
- **Tone**: Visual-first, aspirational, lifestyle-focused

### TikTok
- **Character Limit**: 2,200 characters
- **Optimal Length**: Short and punchy (50-100 characters)
- **Best Practices**: Hook in first 3 seconds, use trending sounds
- **Hashtags**: 3-5 relevant hashtags
- **Tone**: Fun, energetic, authentic

### LinkedIn
- **Character Limit**: 3,000 characters
- **Optimal Length**: 150-300 characters
- **Best Practices**: Professional tone, data-driven, industry insights
- **Hashtags**: 3-5 hashtags
- **Tone**: Professional, informative, value-focused

### LINE OA
- **Character Limit**: 5,000 characters
- **Optimal Length**: 200-500 characters
- **Best Practices**: Direct, personalized, include contact info
- **Hashtags**: Not commonly used
- **Tone**: Direct, helpful, conversational
