## How to Use This Template

### Daily Tasks
1. Log all posts in the Weekly Tracking Sheet
2. Monitor and record initial performance metrics
3. Respond to comments and messages
4. Note any immediate insights or issues

### Weekly Tasks
1. Complete Weekly Performance Summary
2. Update engagement metrics for all posts
3. Calculate engagement rates
4. Review goals vs. actuals
5. Identify top and worst performers
6. Plan content for the following week

### Monthly Tasks
1. Complete Monthly Performance Dashboard
2. Update all comparison trackers
3. Analyze content type performance
4. Review lead generation metrics
5. Compile best/worst performers
6. Write monthly insights and recommendations
7. Set goals for next month
8. Present findings to team/management

### Best Practices
- **Consistency**: Update trackers at the same time daily/weekly
- **Accuracy**: Verify all metrics from official platform analytics
- **Context**: Always add notes to explain unusual patterns
- **Action-Oriented**: Focus on actionable insights, not just data
- **Collaboration**: Share insights with sales and management teams
- **Continuous Improvement**: Use data to refine strategy monthly

### Key Metrics Definitions

**Reach**: Number of unique accounts that saw your content
**Impressions**: Total number of times your content was displayed
**Engagement**: Total interactions (likes, comments, shares, saves, clicks)
**Engagement Rate**: (Total Engagement / Reach) × 100
**Conversion Rate**: (Leads or Sales / Total Reach) × 100
**ROI**: ((Revenue - Cost) / Cost) × 100
