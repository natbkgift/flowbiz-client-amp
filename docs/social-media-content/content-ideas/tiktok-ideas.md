## üí° Pro Tips for TikTok Success

### 1. Hook is EVERYTHING
- First 1-3 seconds determine success
- Use pattern interrupts
- Create curiosity
- Ask shocking questions

### 2. Keep Videos Short
- 15-30 seconds ideal
- TikTok rewards completion rate
- Better short and rewatched than long and skipped

### 3. Add Captions
- 80% watch without sound
- Auto-captions or manual
- Helps with accessibility
- Improves watch time

### 4. Post Consistently
- Algorithm rewards consistency
- Daily posting minimum
- Same time each day if possible

### 5. Engage with Comments
- Reply to every comment in first hour
- Creates conversation
- Signals to algorithm
- Builds community

### 6. Cross-post to Instagram Reels
- Same content, different platform
- Maximum efficiency
- Slightly adjust for each platform

### 7. Watch Your Analytics
- Check after 48 hours
- Learn what works
- Double down on winners
- Stop doing what doesn't work

### 8. Don't Be Too Salesy
- Entertain first, sell second
- Provide value
- Build trust
- Sales will come naturally
