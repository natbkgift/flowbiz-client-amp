## Executive Summary

AMP (Asset Management Property) is an AI-powered real estate management platform targeting the Pattaya property market. We address the critical pain points of traditional property management through intelligent automation, enabling real estate agents to scale their operations efficiently while providing superior customer service.

**Target Market:** Pattaya real estate agencies (5-50 properties)  
**Revenue Model:** SaaS subscription + transaction fees  
**ROI Target:** 3x property management capacity with existing staff  
**Timeline:** 8-week MVP, 6-month full launch

---

## Market Analysis

### Pattaya Real Estate Landscape

**Market Size:**
- 15,000+ active property listings in Pattaya area
- 200+ active real estate agencies
- 50% foreign buyers (international market)
- 20B THB annual transaction volume

**Target Segment:**
- Small-to-medium agencies (5-50 properties)
- Independent agents transitioning to digital
- New agencies seeking competitive advantage
- Existing agencies with manual processes

### Pain Points

**Current Challenges:**
1. **Manual Multi-platform Management** - Agents manually post to 5-8 platforms
2. **Lead Response Time** - Average 4-6 hours to respond (losing hot leads)
3. **Communication Overload** - 100+ LINE messages daily
4. **Poor Lead Tracking** - Excel-based, no scoring or automation
5. **Limited Analytics** - No market insights or performance tracking
6. **Tour Scheduling Chaos** - Double bookings, no-shows
7. **Document Bottlenecks** - Manual contract generation

**Business Impact:**
- 30% lead loss due to slow response
- 40% time wasted on repetitive tasks
- 60% of agents can't scale beyond 20 properties
- Limited market intelligence for pricing decisions

## Business Model

### Revenue Streams

**1. SaaS Subscription (Primary)**
- **Starter:** 2,900 THB/month (1 user, 10 properties)
- **Professional:** 5,900 THB/month (3 users, 30 properties)
- **Agency:** 11,900 THB/month (10 users, 100 properties)
- **Enterprise:** Custom pricing (50+ users, unlimited properties)

**2. Transaction Fees (Secondary)**
- 0.5% on closed deals facilitated through platform
- Optional, only charged on successful transactions
- Capped at 20,000 THB per transaction

**3. Premium Features (Future)**
- Virtual tour enhancements (3D models)
- Advanced market analytics reports
- Custom integration development
- White-label solutions

### Cost Structure

**Development Phase (Months 1-6):**
- AI/ML development: 150,000 THB
- Platform integration: 80,000 THB
- LINE Bot setup: 40,000 THB
- Testing & QA: 60,000 THB
- **Total Development:** 330,000 THB

**Operating Costs (Monthly):**
- Cloud infrastructure (FlowBiz VPS): 8,000 THB
- LINE API & messaging: 3,000 THB
- Platform API fees: 5,000 THB
- Maintenance & support: 12,000 THB
- **Total Monthly:** 28,000 THB

**Break-even Analysis:**
- Target: 10 paying customers (Month 3)
- Revenue at break-even: 45,000 THB/month
- Expected break-even: Month 5

## Risk Analysis

### Technical Risks

**HIGH:** Platform API changes
- **Impact:** Integration breaks, manual posting required
- **Mitigation:** Multi-platform redundancy, fallback manual mode
- **Contingency:** 48-hour response SLA, alternative platforms

**MEDIUM:** LINE API rate limits
- **Impact:** Message delays during peak hours
- **Mitigation:** Message queuing, intelligent batching
- **Contingency:** Upgrade LINE API tier, optimize message flow

**LOW:** AI model accuracy
- **Impact:** Poor lead scoring, incorrect responses
- **Mitigation:** Human-in-loop validation, continuous training
- **Contingency:** Manual override always available

### Business Risks

**HIGH:** Market competition
- **Impact:** International players entering Pattaya market
- **Mitigation:** Hyperlocal focus, Thai-first approach, personal service
- **Contingency:** Partner/acquisition by larger player

**MEDIUM:** Slow adoption
- **Impact:** Extended time to break-even
- **Mitigation:** Aggressive beta program, free trial, referral incentives
- **Contingency:** Reduce burn rate, extend runway

**LOW:** Regulatory changes
- **Impact:** New real estate tech regulations
- **Mitigation:** Compliance monitoring, legal consultation
- **Contingency:** Platform adjustments, feature modifications

## Appendix B: Customer Journey Map

### Pre-Purchase (Awareness)
**Touchpoints:** Facebook ads, referral, expo booth  
**Agent Need:** Easy trial signup  
**AMP Response:** 14-day free trial, no credit card

### Trial (Evaluation)
**Touchpoints:** Onboarding call, tutorial videos, support  
**Agent Need:** Quick wins, see value  
**AMP Response:** Assisted first listing, LINE bot setup, sample data

**Touchpoints:** Usage data, ROI calculator, upgrade prompt
